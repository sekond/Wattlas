02 / 12 · PROBLEM

Electricity has no warehouse

Its price swings hour to hour: cheap when the wind blows, dear when it doesn't. The data is open, but raw. It is timezone-aware, gap-ridden, and spread across a dozen national APIs. And the well-known maps stop at the country line, where the physics begins.

The signal is public. Reading it honestly is the work.

10 / 12 · ISOLATION

Each source fails on its own

Every data source is its own module — different auth, units and language. A failure in one can't break the others; a missing key degrades to an honest "awaiting source" state.

ENTSO-E prices, generation, flows
NESO GB carbon + constraints
MaStR every DE installation
SMARD control-area balance
ODRÉ French régional
netztransparenz curtailment

The non-obvious traps are written down as "landmines" and coded around: the Oct-2025 resolution break, the 23/25-hour DST days, the zones that aren't administrative regions.

Built to fail open, one source at a time.
