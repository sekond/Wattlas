Illustrative central values (€/MWh). Real ranges are wide and assumption-dependent: solar full-cost ≈ 60–100, wind ≈ 55–95, existing FR fleet ≈ 50–70, new nuclear ≈ 100–190 (swings most with cost of capital). System costs rise with renewable share; back-end costs are large in total (Cigéo ≈ €33–37bn) but small per MWh spread over decades, and are provisioned, not ignored. Sources: Lazard LCOE+ 2024 · Cour des comptes (EPR) · ANDRA / Cigéo · OECD-NEA system costs · IRENA. Not a live feed — curated published estimates; takes no side.
